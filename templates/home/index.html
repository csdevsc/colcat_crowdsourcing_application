What is ColCat?

ColCat is a comprehensive project aiming to digitize handwritten color categorization data. Learn more about the project here.

What is crowdsourcing?

Crowdsourcing allows us to distribute tasks over the internet and enlist the help of people like you to help us with our project! To see a sample of what a task is, please visit the demo.

Why should I participate?

By participating, you will be helping us develop a collaborative research platform for investigating human color categorization behaviors across ethnolinguistic groups!

Will I get paid?

Participating in our crowdsourcing research from this page is voluntary. To be paid for your efforts, you can participate in our studies on Mechanical Turk.

How long does a task take?

Currently, each task is designed to be completed in approximately 40-60 minutes. Some participants have been able to complete our tasks within 20 minutes, and some take longer.
